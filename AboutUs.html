About Kibellan Universal Solutions

Your trusted partner in healthcare supply chain management

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Kibellan Universal Solutions has grown from a small renal consumables supplier to a global healthcare solutions provider. Our mission has always been to ensure that quality healthcare products are accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We partner with manufacturers, healthcare providers, and NGOs to create efficient supply chains that deliver critical dialysis consumables supplies where they're needed most.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted healthcare supply partner, enabling better health outcomes through reliable access to quality renal consumables.

Our Mission

To provide healthcare institutions with reliable, affordable, and high-quality renal consumables supplies through efficient logistics and ethical business practices.

Our Values
