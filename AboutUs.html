About Kibellan Universal Solutions

Your trusted partner in healthcare supply chain management

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Kibellan Universal Solutions has grown from a small medical supplier to a global healthcare solutions provider. Our mission has always been to ensure that quality healthcare products are accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We partner with manufacturers, healthcare providers, and NGOs to create efficient supply chains that deliver critical medical supplies where they're needed most.
